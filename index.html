Why Eat Fruits?

Fruits are nature's ultimate fast food. Packed with essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber, they provide a clean and sustainable source of energy.

A diet rich in diverse fruits can help lower blood pressure, reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke, prevent some types of cancer, and lower the risk of eye and digestive problems.

What to Take Daily

  • Aim for Variety: Eat the rainbow. Different colors provide different nutrients.
  • Portion Size: The AHA recommends 4 servings of fruit per day (e.g., 1 medium apple = 1 serving).
  • Morning Energy: Bananas and apples are excellent for sustained morning energy.
  • Post-Workout: Watermelon and oranges help replenish fluids and glycogen stores.
